Mod Name: Quick potion
Mod Category: Item/Reusable
Description: If you are too lazy to prepare potions, you can use this mod.
Minimum price for a red drink (10) + Minimum price for a purple drink (10) = 20 flames.  Enchant red (10) + purple (10) = 20 essences * 2 (Fictional price for lust) = 40 essences.
